Poha (flattened rice) – 1 cup

Oil – 2 tbsp

Mustard seeds (rai) – ½ tsp

Cumin seeds (jeera) – ½ tsp (optional)

Green chili – 1, chopped

Onion – 1 medium, finely chopped

Potato – 1 small, diced (optional but common in Kanda Batata Poha)

Curry leaves – 6–8

Turmeric powder (haldi) – ¼ tsp

Salt – to taste

Sugar – ½ tsp (optional, gives nice balance)

Lemon juice – 1 tsp (adjust to taste)

Fresh coriander (cilantro) – 2 tbsp, chopped

Roasted peanuts – 2 tbsp (for crunch)
